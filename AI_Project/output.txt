            About us
           
              SpryIQ Technologies is a leading provider for solutions in Product Development and Testing Services, Cloud Infrastructure Services, DevOps, Security Automation(SOAR). SpyrIQ enables organizations to leverage the years of investment they have made in their application assets, deliver more from IT budgets and reduce the risk of implementing new technologies. For our start-up customers, we understand our client's business realities, ambitions and challenges and help them achieve their goals by providing cost effective and high quality services.
 
SpryIQ Technologies is a trusted partner for comprehensive FortiSOAR professional services. We excel in Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response (SOAR) and offer implementation and management of FortiSOAR cyber technology. We provide integration services with platforms like Splunk, ServiceNow, SEIM, Securonix, and developing custom content including dashboards, playbooks, and executive reports. Our solutions empower organizations to enhance their security operations, automate workflows, and respond effectively to ever increasing threats. Connect with us to safeguard your organization's digital assets and navigate the complex cybersecurity landscape with confidence.
 
SpryIQ Technologies offers array of services based on cloud such as cloud readiness assessment, cloud architecture and design, cloud migration,  cloud infrastructure management, cloud compliance and security, container orchestration in cloud, DevOps and automation and testing services in cloud. We help our customers move to appropriate target cloud vendor platform (AWS, Azure, Google, Heroku), deployment option (Public, Private, Hybrid), suitable type of service (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) and the appropriate level of remediation.
